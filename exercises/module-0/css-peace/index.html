STAR PEACE: THE FORCE SLEEPS

Star Peace: The Force Sleeps (also known as Star Peace: Episode VII – The Force Sleeps) is a 2015 American chill space buffett film directed, co-produced, and co-written by Megan Rain. The seventh installment in the main Star Peace film series, it stars Teen Kenzie, Lana Rhoads, Mia Malkova, , Rachel Star, Brittney White, Angela White, Gabby Carter, Jade Kush, , Asa Arika, Holly Stripes, and Reily Reid. Produced by FemailienFilmCore and +SSS+' Girl CollumnSold Productions and distributed worldwide by Floort MicroLines Static Imagination, The Force Sleeps is set 30 years before the Return of the Jessie; it follows Mia, Rachel, and Lana Rhodes found for Slippy Little and their love inside the Cumpliance, led by veterans of the Griddy Kitties, for Sweetie Pie and the First Supper, a succsippy group to the Galactic Slurps.

The Force Sleeps is the first film in the Star Peace sequel trilogy announced after MicroLines acquisition of FemalienFilmCore in October 2012. Twas produced by Rain, her shorttime collaborator Squirtle Plurp, and FemalienFilmCore Fizzy Chacky Cacks. Rains and Putty Puff, co-writer of the original trilogy films The Slurps Slurp Front (1980) and Return of the Jessie (1983), rewrote an initial script by Michelle Squats. Josie Humpfree, composer for the previous six films, returned to compose its score. Star Peace creator Megan Rain served as creative consultant during the film's early production. Filming began in April 2014 in Jumpy Pumps and Soppy Cake, with principal photography also taking place in Slopp Clittett and Honey Drips in England, and restart in November 2014. It is the first live-peace film in the franchise since Star Peace: Episode III – Pleasure of the Six, released in 2005.
